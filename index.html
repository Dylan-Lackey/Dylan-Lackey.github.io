About us

Departures is a growing Canadian tech inspired delivery company specializing in same day and overnight delivery for packages under 40lbs and less then 2 cubic meters. We have 10,000 employees across Canada, we are organized with a variety of departments to form a professional level organization. Our focus is to use todays current online web technology to make customers shipping and delivery needs the quickest, easiest and most reliable service to date.
